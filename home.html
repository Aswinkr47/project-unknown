ITS ALL ABOUT MYLADUMPARA SHAJI

SHAJI is a self-proclaimed sorcerer supreme who possesses immense magical power, yet often forgets the simplest of spells due to his surprisingly dumb nature. Despite his flaws, he roams the land with confidence, accompanied by his two majestic pet peacocks who seem far wiser than their master. Known for accidentally turning rocks into chickens and occasionally himself into a frog, Shaji still believes he is destined for greatness. Deep down, however, his heart is pure, and when things truly matter, even his chaotic magic somehow finds a way to save the day.
