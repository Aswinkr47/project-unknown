ITS ALL ABOUT KICH KICH

LORE

Kishore, better known as Kichu, was always known as the smart one—sharp-minded, kind-hearted, and effortlessly friendly. But that same innocence became his weakness. People mistook his kindness for naivety, and slowly, he became the target of endless jokes and trolling. Still, he smiled through it… until things began to change. Every night, after midnight—his usual sleeping time—he started hearing strange, unexplainable sounds outside his house. Whispers. Footsteps. Knocks that no one else could hear.
At first, he ignored it. But as days passed, the sounds grew louder, more personal… almost like they were calling him. A singer by passion and an actor by talent, Kichu began to blur the line between reality and performance. The boy everyone once laughed at was no longer the same. The trolling didn’t break him—it awakened something darker. And slowly, silently… Kishore began writing his own story—not as the hero everyone ignored, but as the villain no one saw coming.
As the nights grew heavier, Kishore stopped fearing the sounds—he started listening to them. The whispers no longer felt random; they spoke in patterns, revealing secrets… about people around him. The same people who once laughed at him. One by one, he began to notice their hidden fears, their lies, their weaknesses. It was as if the darkness outside his house had chosen him, guiding him. By day, Kichu was still the same cheerful, harmless guy everyone knew—cracking jokes, singing casually, blending in. But by night, he transformed. His acting skills became his greatest weapon, masking the storm building inside him. His voice, once used for songs, now echoed in empty rooms, rehearsing something far more sinister. No one noticed the shift. No one realized that the boy they once mocked was quietly pulling the strings. The sounds outside were no longer just noises—they were instructions. And Kishore was no longer a victim of the story. He was becoming its author. And this time… there would be no laughter.
